Frontend Development

This form of development is among the most used and oftentimes the most important aspect when building sites. Frontend dev covers what the user sees and how they interact with your website. Great implementation of this goes to create a seemless user interface, making the user feel familar and comfortable with navigating the content already.

Although this is crucial to a website, this aspect of a site can often be highly neglected leaving the user lost and confused when exploring through the pages. Many websites don't take enough time to consider how their visitors will navgiate through the website and instead throw the content in a slightly traversable way. This has a negative impression on the viewer as it takes up more of their time and potentially leaves them frustrated and annoyed.

As a freelance developer I often focus the majority of my effort into frontend web-development. This form of development is often what is neccessary as I develop website for mostly single clients. Due to this neccessity I make sure to offer that seemless UI that only exceptional frontend development offers.
